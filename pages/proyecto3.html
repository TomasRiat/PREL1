TDD Method / Organize your team

Emma Johnson

This was one of my first personal projects back in 2019, just when I started a research based on the organizing methods available for managing a big team of people working on the same project. After about 2 months of research, I came to the conclusion that making things simple is the best way in this type of cases. I then developed an oganization method - the TDD Method.

To do

  • make a list based in every single thing you have to take care of in order to complete big tasks.
  • he more the better - creating little tasks helps you work more efficiently and mark items as done more quickly.
  • don’t overdue, just complete what is told and avoid adding more stuff.

Doing

  • once you start working on something, you can move it to the “doing” column.
  • your team can easily know what you are doing simply by checking the list. this makes communication more efficient and to the point.
  • seting timers can help you motivated and focused while you do the assigned task.

Done

  • once you finished with your assigned tasks, you are finally able yo mark it as done! This helps you stay motivated by showing you how much was made.
  • having a “done” list makes you able to analize what has been done in a period of time.
  • completing all the tasks means time to relax! :)
